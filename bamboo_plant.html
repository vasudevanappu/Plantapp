Bamboo Plant

Bamboo is a fast-growing perennial plant belonging to the grass family Poaceae. It is known for its tall, woody stems and can grow up to 91 cm (35 inches) in a single day. Bamboo is widely used for construction, furniture, paper, and as a food source in the form of bamboo shoots.

Scientific Name Bambusoideae
Average Lifespan Several decades (depending on species)
Height Up to 30 meters
Native Region Asia, Africa, Americas
Uses Construction, furniture, paper, food (bamboo shoots)
Special Feature Can grow more than 90 cm in a single day
